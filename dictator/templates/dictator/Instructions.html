You have completed Part 1 of the experiment - the Dice Roll game.
Now, we ask you to complete the Dictatorship game. You will play only {{Constants.num_rounds}} round.

Game Rules:

  • In this task you will be paired with another participant of the experiment randomly.
  • Each of you will receive endowment of {{Constants.endowment}} for this task.
  • Each of you will be randomly assigned to one of the two roles : 1) Dictator or 2) Receiver.
  • Depending on the role you will be assigned you will have to perform different tasks.
Dictator's Role:
If you have been assigned Dictator's role, you will have to decide to add or subtract money from Receiver's earnings.
Your decision will not affect your payment. That is, you will receive your initial endowment of {{Constants.endowment}}.
However, your decision will affect Receiver's payment in the following way:
  1. If you decide to subtract from Receiver's earnings, the amount he will be paid in the end would be equal to {{Constants.endowment}} - [the amount you set].
  2. If you decide to add to Receiver's earnings, the amount he will be paid in the end would be equal to {{Constants.endowment}} + [the amount you set].
Receiver's Role:
If you have been assigned to Receiver's role you will have to wait for Dictator's decision. You payoff would depend on Dictator's decision.

If you have read and understood the rules of the game, proceed by clicking Next button.
